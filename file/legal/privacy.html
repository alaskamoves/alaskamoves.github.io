Privacy Policy

This site, alaskamoves.us, does not use cookies, trackers, or third-party analytics. We do not embed marketing beacons, fingerprint your browser, or store behavioural logs for analysis.

Voluntary submissions (via third-party platforms) may include your name, contact details, and message content. These are retained only for the purpose of communication and are never shared, sold, or analyzed.

Server-level logs may collect standard metadata (IP address, user-agent, time of access) for basic security and uptime diagnostics. These logs are not correlated with identities and are purged regularly.

By using this site, you acknowledge and accept that we aim to minimize all data collection and actively avoid surveillance paradigms common elsewhere on the internet.
